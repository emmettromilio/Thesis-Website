What is synapse?

Synapse was built for the moment most tools are missing. The space where you're in production mode and the path forward isn't clear yet. Not for the messy, exciting first steps of bringing something new into the world.

THE PROBLEM

Modern product teams face constant production pressure from Figma and tools that want to help you get to the idea in the first place. At just steps the human part entirely. Nobody was building for the blank page.

How it works

OUR SOLUTION

Synapse is a structured ideation workspace that gives you room to think. Whether you work best visually or in lists, love a blank canvas or Figtree mode, or challenge yourself with a timed Guided Session — Either way, every idea on that board came from you.

WHY IT MATTERS

The earliest stages of a project sets the tone for everything that follows. When that foundation is strong — when you actually understand your concept before you start building it — the work that comes later is infinitely more fluid.

Synapse exists to make that foundation something you can build on.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

We made one rule when building Synapse: it will never generate ideas for you. We built tools that support the process—not replace it. The tools that support that process should strengthen your instincts, not replace them.
